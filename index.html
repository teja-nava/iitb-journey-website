My IIT Bombay Journey

From stepping into the monumental campus gates to becoming a part of a vibrant and intellectually intense ecosystem, my journey at IIT Bombay has been nothing short of transformative. Each day has been a blend of challenge, exploration, growth, and endless learning. Whether it was late-night hostel discussions, hands-on civil engineering projects, or participating in Mood Indigo — every experience helped me evolve not just as a student, but as a well-rounded human being.

Timeline / Milestones

📅 Day 1: Orientation at LT auditorium — awe and excitement.
🎉 Mood Indigo: Hospitality volunteer, learned teamwork & chaos handling!
📖 CE213: My favorite course — tough, but rewarding.
💼 First internship: Learned real-world applications of my classes.

What I Learned

  • 📐 Gained deep technical knowledge in structural engineering and materials.
  • 🧠 Learned to work under pressure, manage time, and balance academics with extracurriculars.
  • 👥 Developed strong team collaboration skills through group projects and fest organization.
  • 🌱 Embraced self-discipline and built a mindset of lifelong learning and curiosity.
  • 📣 Improved public speaking and presentation skills through seminars and events.
